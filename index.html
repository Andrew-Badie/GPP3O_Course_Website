About the Program

Dive into the transformative journey of our all new Service & Leadership Team (SaLT) for our Grade 9 youth, the ultimate stepping stone between senior camper and future counsellor! Registration includes daily hot lunch & two snacks, field trips, service trips, leadership training, camp t-shirts, and much more! This unique intermediate stage allows SaLT participants to partake in the exhilarating senior camp field trips and the unforgettable go-away camp week. As SaLT members, these young leaders will also have the distinct honour of engaging in impactful volunteer opportunities during the Junior Camp, nurturing a sense of responsibility and leadership. This program is designed to enhance their leadership skills, build mentorship qualities, and deepen their connection with their peers.
